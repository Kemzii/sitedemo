Who We Are


Just Call Courier service was founded in 2011. We are a courier service that delivers documents, small and large packages, such as: letters, utility bills, event tickets, invitations, invoices, tax office transactions, banking documents, auto parts, groceries and meals. They implement a business to consumer business model. As a courier service they aim to provide reliable, fast and efficient delivers to individual customers as well as deliveries to other companies.
